The Litany Against Gurus

Eliezer Yudkowsky, 18 December 2007 08:11PM

I am your hero!
I am your master!
Learn my arts,
Seek my way.

Learn as I learned,
Seek as I sought.

Envy me!
Aim at me!
Rival me!
Transcend me!

Look back,
Smile,
And then—
Eyes front!

I was never your city,
Just a stretch of your road.

Bay Area Events Roundup

Eliezer Yudkowsky, 24 July 2010 06:04AM

This Saturday (i.e., the 24th, that is, tomorrow) is the peak of the Floating Festival [http://lesswrong.com/lw/2hi/floating_festival_jul_2225/].

Michael Vassar says: "I'm going to be speaking tomorrow (= Saturday July 24th) at 4PM at Bay Area Mensa, in Mountain View, on the scientific method, the history of science, and how to think rationally about most scientific controversies including the Singularity. Less Wrongers are invited to attend. Interested people should email David Verdirame [mailto:david.verdirame@gmail.com]."

The Open Science Summit [http://opensciencesummit.com/schedule/] is July 29-31, in Berkeley.

And as ever, the Singularity Summit [http://www.singularitysummit.com/] approaches on August 14-15 in San Francisco. Now featuring James Randi, Irene Pepperberg, and John Tooby.

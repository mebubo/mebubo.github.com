Rolf Nelson's "The Rational Entrepreneur"

Eliezer Yudkowsky, 04 November 2009 09:31PM

New blog up by longtime OB/LWer Rolf Nelson, "The Rational Entrepreneur [http://www.rolfnelson.com/]" at rolfnelson.com [http://www.rolfnelson.com/]. On "the overlap between entrepreneurship and the modern tools of rationality". Rolf will post daily through November, and after that it will depend on how much traction the blog gets.

First posts: Pay more attention to the statistics! [http://www.rolfnelson.com/2009/11/pay-more-attention-to-statistics.html] (which say to do what you know, not what you love) and Having co-founders is valuable but not crucial [http://www.rolfnelson.com/2009/11/having-co-founders-is-valuable-but-not.html] (according to a study).

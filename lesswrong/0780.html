Singularity Institute $100K Challenge Grant / 2009 Donations Reminder

Eliezer Yudkowsky, 30 December 2009 12:36AM

In case you missed the notice at the SIAI Blog [http://intelligence.org/blog/2009/12/23/announcing-the-2010-singularity-research-challenge/], the Singularity Institute's next Challenge Grant [http://intelligence.org/challenge] is now running - $100,000 of matching funds for all donations until February 28th, 2010. If you want your donation to be tax-deductible in the U.S. for 2009, donations postmarked by December 31st will be counted for this tax year.
